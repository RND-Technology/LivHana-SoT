### Raffle System

```
GET  /api/raffles                      ✅ List raffles
GET  /api/raffles/:raffleId            ✅ Raffle details
POST /api/raffles/:raffleId/purchase   ✅ Buy tickets
POST /api/raffles/:raffleId/draw       ✅ Draw winner
GET  /api/raffles/stats                ✅ Raffle statistics
```
