#### Hierarchy

**Primary Text** (Most Important):
- Hook statement (first 3 seconds)
- Key takeaway
- Statistics/numbers
- Call-to-action

**Secondary Text** (Supporting):
- Captions for dialogue
- Context information
- Definitions
- Sources

**Tertiary Text** (Branding):
- Episode numbers
- Logo/series name
- Social handles
- Hashtags (minimal on-screen)
